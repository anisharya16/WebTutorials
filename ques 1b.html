Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of many favourite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1tbs Butter
  • 2tbs cream

Methods

  1. Melt Butter in a frying pan over medium heat.
  2. gently mix the eggs and the cream in the bowl.
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. using a spatula fold the eggs from the egde of the pan to the centre every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omlette).
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
